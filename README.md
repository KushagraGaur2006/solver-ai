# 🎓 Solver AI

**Solver AI** is an intelligent, interactive AI assistant designed specifically to help high school students master Mathematics and Science. Built with a focus on pedagogy, Solver AI doesn't just hand out answers—it guides students through complex problem-solving steps, fostering deeper conceptual understanding.



## 🚀 Features

* **Step-by-Step Guidance:** Breaks down complex math equations and scientific principles into manageable, easy-to-understand steps.
* **Multi-Disciplinary Support:** Covers high school level Algebra, Calculus, Geometry, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
* **Interactive Gradio UI:** A clean, intuitive, and mobile-friendly interface designed for seamless student interaction.
* **Socratic Approach:** Encourages critical thinking by prompting students with hints rather than just final solutions (customizable based on model prompting).
